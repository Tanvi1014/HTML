Goa - Beaches & Nightlife

⏱️ 4 min read

Goa is India’s ultimate beach destination. Sun, sand, and nightlife make it a paradise for travelers. Perfect for solo travelers and couples alike.

  • Best time: Nov to Feb
  • Budget: ₹5,000 - ₹12,000
  • Don’t miss: Baga beach and flea markets
  • Sunscreen
  • Swimwear
  • Hat & Sunglasses

Kashmir - Heaven on Earth

⏱️ 5 min read

Kashmir offers stunning landscapes, snow-capped mountains, and serene lakes. Ideal for nature lovers and honeymooners.

  • Best time: Mar to Oct
  • Budget: ₹10,000 - ₹20,000
  • Don’t miss: Dal Lake & Gulmarg
  • Warm clothes
  • Camera
  • Travel insurance

Dubai - Luxury on a Budget

⏱️ 4 min read

Dubai offers modern architecture, luxury shopping, and desert adventures. It’s a city of dreams for urban explorers.

  • Best time: Nov to Mar
  • Budget: ₹25,000 - ₹50,000
  • Don’t miss: Burj Khalifa & Desert Safari
  • Comfortable shoes
  • Sun protection
  • Valid passport & visa

Maldives - Tropical Paradise

⏱️ 5 min read

Maldives is famous for crystal clear waters and overwater bungalows. Ideal for honeymooners and luxury travelers.

  • Best time: Nov to Apr
  • Budget: ₹40,000 - ₹80,000
  • Don’t miss: Snorkeling & Island Hopping
  • Swimwear
  • Snorkeling gear
  • Sunscreen

Paris - Romantic Getaway

⏱️ 4 min read

Paris is the city of love. With historic architecture, cafes, and art, it’s a perfect destination for couples and culture lovers.

  • Best time: Apr to Jun
  • Budget: ₹30,000 - ₹60,000
  • Don’t miss: Eiffel Tower & Louvre Museum
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Camera
  • French phrasebook or app
