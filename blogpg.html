My Morning Routine

Every morning, I like to start my day with a few simple habits that keep me focused and motivated. Here are the main things to do I do before starting work:

  • Drink a glass of water
  • Do 10 minutes of meditation
  • Go for a short walk
  • Plan the tasks for the day

Once I’ve completed my morning routine, I move on to my top priorities for the day. Here’s the order I follow to stay productive:

  1. Check and reply to important emails
  2. Work on the main project or coding task
  3. Attend team meetings or discussions
  4. Spend some time learning new skills

That’s my typical morning! Maintaining this routine helps me stay consistent and positive.
