Explore India’s Best Destinations

Plan unforgettable trips across India at the most affordable rates.

Jaipur

The Pink City famous for palaces, forts, and royal history.

Best Time: Oct-Feb | Budget: ₹10k-15k

Manali

Snow-capped mountains, adventure sports and beautiful landscapes.

Best Time: Oct-Mar | Budget: ₹8k-12k

Kerala

Backwaters, beaches and serene landscapes with cultural richness.

Best Time: Nov-Feb | Budget: ₹12k-18k

Varanasi

Spiritual city along the Ganges with rich culture and temples.

Best Time: Oct-Mar | Budget: ₹5k-10k

Rishikesh

Adventure, yoga retreats, and river rafting in the Himalayas.

Best Time: Sep-Mar | Budget: ₹6k-12k

Travel Tips for National Trips

  • Book early to get the best rates on transport and hotels.
  • Check weather and pack accordingly.
  • Respect local culture and customs.
  • Carry light cash and travel-friendly cards.
  • Use public transport or local taxis to save money.
